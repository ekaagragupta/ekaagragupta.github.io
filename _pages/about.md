I am a bachelor’s (computer science and engineering Specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning ) student at MUJ (Manipal University Jaipur) graduating in 2028 . At the moment as a pre-final year undergraduate , I am interning with the MNIT (Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur) , where 
I work on a modular Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline - implementing and optimizing large-scale inference workflows using <strong>vLLM</strong>, Hugging Face Transformers, and PyTorch on NVIDIA GPUs.

Before joining MNIT, I was an AI/ML Intern at <strong>CoreGen</strong>, where I worked on satellite image super-resolution with a full-stack MERN dashboard for visualizing reconstruction outputs and explored lightweight object detection (MobileNet SSD) with real-time anomaly detection. I am also quite fond of teaching courses and technical workshops, broadly on Generative AI , Retrieval Systems and  software engineering (/teaching).

Apart from the experiences above, I have also dabbled with functional competitive programming [leetcode](https://leetcode.com/u/ekaagragupta/)
 and type + proof theory, more explainable ai models , applied machine learning (for science and social good), technical blog writing for ML frameworks, and   build and maintain a few research-driven systems of my own — <strong>SEVAS</strong>, a satellite-based environmental violation detection system; <strong>GETHER</strong>, an explainable AQI forecasting framework (patent filed, under examination); and <strong>MemoRAG</strong>, a memory-augmented RAG framework for long-context retrieval projects
 
 I am currently interested in Machine Learning Engineering (Computer Vision and HCI, Edge AI, Systems for ML, Physics-aware ML) and High-Performance Agentic multi-models. I advocate for and am extremely passionate about research	 and open-science.
