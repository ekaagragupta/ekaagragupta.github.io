## IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (IEEE CIS)


Served as the **Joint Head of Sponsorship & Curation** for the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (IEEE CIS) Student Chapter at Manipal University Jaipur, contributing to the planning and execution of technical events, workshops, and community initiatives.

In this leadership role, I collaborated with cross-functional teams to establish industry partnerships, curate high-quality technical content, and ensure the successful delivery of AI and technology-focused events. The position required balancing strategic outreach with event planning to create engaging learning experiences for students.

### Key Responsibilities

- Secured sponsorships through outreach to startups, technology companies, and industry partners.
- Curated technical content for workshops, hackathons, speaker sessions, and flagship IEEE events.
- Coordinated with speakers, mentors, and organizing committees to streamline event execution.
- Planned event timelines, logistics, and promotional strategies to maximize student engagement.
- Worked closely with the design, marketing, and technical teams to deliver high-quality community events.
- Assisted in identifying emerging topics in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Generative AI, and Software Engineering for technical sessions.
- Strengthened collaborations with student organizations and external communities to expand participation and outreach.

### Impact

- Contributed to the successful organization of multiple IEEE technical events and workshops.
- Built relationships with industry partners and sponsors to support chapter initiatives.
- Enhanced the quality and relevance of technical sessions through thoughtful content curation.
- Helped foster a collaborative learning environment within the university's AI and technology community.
