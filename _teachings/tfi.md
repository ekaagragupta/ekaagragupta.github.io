### Key Responsibilities

- Coordinated chapter operations and assisted in planning community outreach initiatives and educational campaigns.
- Managed volunteer onboarding, scheduling, and coordination to ensure smooth execution of chapter activities.
- Collaborated with schools, NGOs, and partner organizations for educational and social impact events.
- Assisted in organizing fundraising drives, awareness campaigns, and volunteer engagement programs.
- Planned event timelines, delegated responsibilities, and monitored progress across organizing teams.
- Maintained communication between executive members, volunteers, and external stakeholders.
- Prepared meeting agendas, documented discussions, and tracked action items to ensure effective chapter management.
- Supported promotional activities through social media, event publicity, and community engagement initiatives.

### Contributions

- Successfully coordinated multiple volunteer-driven educational and community service initiatives.
- Helped streamline communication and operational workflows within the chapter.
- Encouraged active student participation through awareness campaigns and recruitment drives.
- Worked closely with leadership teams to improve event planning, volunteer management, and execution efficiency.
- Contributed to creating an inclusive and collaborative environment focused on educational equity and social impact.
